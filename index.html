Scan QR to Contact Vehicle Owner

Bharat.io is a simple QR based system that allows anyone to contact a vehicle owner instantly during parking problems or emergency situations, without displaying phone numbers openly on the vehicle.

See How It Works

How It Works

Each vehicle has a unique QR sticker placed on it. The vehicle owner scans the QR once to connect their contact details. After registration, anyone can scan the QR to reach the owner during parking issues or emergencies.

1

Scan the QR sticker and register your vehicle once.

2

Paste the QR sticker on your car, bike, or vehicle.

3

Anyone can scan the QR to contact you when needed.

About Bharat.io

Bharat.io was created to solve a very common problem faced by vehicle owners in cities and public places. Wrong parking and emergency situations often require quick communication, but writing a personal phone number on a vehicle is not always safe.

Our goal is to provide a simple, privacy-friendly solution where vehicle owners remain reachable only when it truly matters. With Bharat.io, a single QR scan helps people connect responsibly.

Privacy & Responsible Use

Bharat.io respects user privacy. Contact details are shared voluntarily by the vehicle owner and are visible only when someone scans the specific QR sticker placed on the vehicle.

This service is strictly meant for parking assistance and emergency communication. Misuse, spam calls, or promotional use of this system is not supported or encouraged.
